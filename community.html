Community

Community Engagement and Involvement

At its core, pyWBE is about fostering a dynamic and collaborative community. In these early stages, we actively welcome contributions and engagement from individuals passionate about wastewater-based epidemiology (WBE). Our aim is to create a space where collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing are at the forefront.

Communication and Collaboration Opportunities

Community Building: Being a new initiative, pyWBE is laying the groundwork for a strong, inclusive community. We encourage those interested in WBE to join us in shaping the direction and growth of this group.

Regular Meetings and Discussions: As we expand our team, regular virtual meetings and discussions are held to brainstorm ideas, discuss project developments, and share expertise. These meetings are crucial for guiding the project's trajectory and ensuring alignment with our collective vision.

Collaboration Platforms: We utilize platforms like Slack and GitHub for ongoing collaboration and communication. These tools facilitate project management, issue tracking, and open discussions, keeping the team connected and engaged.

Engaging with the Wider Community: pyWBE aims to extend its reach beyond existing teams by participating in relevant meetups, conferences, and workshops. These events are opportunities for networking, learning, and showcasing our work to a broader audience.

Contribute to pyWBE: We welcome contributions from all, regardless of expertise level. Whether you're a coder, researcher, student, or WBE enthusiast, your input and involvement can significantly impact pyWBE's development and success.

Join the pyWBE Journey

As pyWBE takes its first steps, we invite you to be part of this exciting journey. Join us to contribute to the evolving field of WBE, share your insights, and collaborate on innovative solutions. Together, we can build a vibrant community and drive forward the advancements in wastewater-based epidemiology.
