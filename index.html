pyWBE - Python Library for wastewater-based Epidemiology

pyWBE stands at the forefront of public health innovation, offering a groundbreaking Python library designed for wastewater-based epidemiology. It's a versatile tool that revolutionizes data analysis, making vital health insights more accessible and actionable for both researchers and practitioners.

Advanced Data Analytics

pyWBE excels in transforming complex wastewater data into insightful analytics, making it invaluable for epidemiological research.

Robust Statistical Modeling

pyWBE integrates cutting-edge statistical models, providing precise and reliable analysis for epidemiological data interpretation.

Scalable Solutions

Catering to diverse datasets, pyWBE offers scalable solutions, ideal for both small-scale studies and large-scale epidemiological research.
