Python Library for Waste-water based Epidemiology

About Us

pyWBE is developed and maintained by the WBE computational team at the Discovery Partners Institute, Chicago

Enhancing Wastewater Analysis with pyWBE

Introducing pyWBE, a pioneering open-source Python library developed by the WBE computational team at the Discovery Partners Institute, Chicago. This library is tailored to streamline wastewater-based epidemiology (WBE), making it more accessible and efficient. As a key resource, pyWBE is instrumental in advancing the vital task of public health monitoring through the analysis of wastewater from communities and facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission with pyWBE is straightforward: to provide a versatile resource that enhances public health decision-making. The library is designed to be user-friendly, addressing the needs of both professionals and students in epidemiology. By simplifying WBE data analysis, pyWBE aims to contribute significantly to public health.

Join the pyWBE Community

PyWBE is built on community contributions and we invite participation from all. Whether you're a student beginning your academic journey, a developer eager to contribute skills, or a researcher in epidemiology, your involvement is valuable. Your insights, code contributions, and data are crucial in the development and enhancement of pyWBE.

Connect and Contribute

Are you interested in contributing to pyWBE or keen to learn more? Explore our contribution guidelines or contact us directly for more details. Join us in our commitment to advancing public health through technological innovation and collaborative efforts.
